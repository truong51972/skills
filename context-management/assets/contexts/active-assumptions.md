# Active Assumptions

Store durable assumptions that future sessions must preserve.

## Assumptions

- Add assumptions only when they affect future work and are still current.

## Constraints

- Add durable constraints, defaults, or operating limits here when they affect future work.

## Review Policy

- Remove assumptions when source files or direct user direction supersede them.

## Exclusions

- Do not record decision history.
- Do not keep stale assumptions after source files or user direction supersede them.
